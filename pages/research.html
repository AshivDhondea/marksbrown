Research

In Brief

I work primarily on improving the timing performance of scintillator detectors. Alteration of the reflection properties of the facets of scintillator crystals used reduces the uncertainty this component introduces.

Quantification of the contributions to the scintillator detector time resolution along with methods to counter the contribution of light tranport are the aims of my PhD.
