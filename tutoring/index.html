Tutoring

Physics and Mathematics

Mark is a physicist at University College London completing his PhD in application of photonics to medical imaging. He has taught physics and mathematics at a variety of levels to students across the UK. This is in addition to his own studies resulting in degrees from the universities of Manchester and Cambridge.

In teaching his primary focus is upon identifying the (often few) weak areas of the student. This in combination with building the confidence of the student has been found to be extremely effective.

Mark lives in Herne Hill and when not studying he is often found in his garden tending his vegetables.

Please contact me at tutoring@markbrown.io for any enquiries.
